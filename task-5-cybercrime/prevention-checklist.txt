🔐 Stay Safe Online
Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
Do not click on unknown links or messages
Always verify payment requests before accepting
Use strong passwords for apps
Enable two-factor authentication
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
Regularly check bank statements
💰 UPI-Specific Tips
Never approve “collect requests” from unknown people
Remember: Receiving money does NOT require PIN
📢 Report Cybercrime
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
